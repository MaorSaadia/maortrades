# Legal Review Checklist

The routes `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/refund-policy`, `/disclaimer`, `/cookie-policy`, and `/accessibility` are implementation-specific drafts, not legal advice or a compliance claim.

## Identity and dates

- [ ] Verify legal operator name and `LEGAL_OPERATOR_NAME`.
- [ ] Verify business country and any required registration/contact disclosures.
- [ ] Select and configure one deliberate `LEGAL_EFFECTIVE_DATE`.
- [ ] Configure support and privacy mailboxes.
- [ ] Review governing-law wording; configure it only after advice.

## Policy review

- [ ] Adapt user-rights, retention, international-processing, children, and consumer-rights language to target markets.
- [ ] Review digital-content withdrawal/refund rules and the personal-use license.
- [ ] Review liability limitations without removing non-excludable rights.
- [ ] Confirm provider descriptions match production hosting, Lemon Squeezy, Resend, GA4, and Pinterest configurations.
- [ ] Confirm hashed Pinterest email matching remains absent or update disclosure before deliberately implementing it.
- [ ] Review accessibility statement after manual assistive-technology and PDF audits.
